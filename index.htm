About this Website

This website is designed to help students in the 2nd Stage Group B at Al-Mustansiriyah University keep track of their weekly assignments. Each day of the week has its own section where students can find their tasks, deadlines, and important updates related to their courses.

The goal of the website is to simplify communication between professors and students by providing an organized platform for assignment distribution and schedule management. Explore each day by clicking the buttons below to see what assignments you have for the week.
